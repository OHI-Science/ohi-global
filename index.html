An Ocean Health Index assessment for Global

Project

The Global Health Index (BHI) is a regional study applying the Ocean Health Index framework. The aim is to initiate the development of a tool that can be used by decision-makers to guide management of the Baltic Sea region towards increased sustainability.

Our team

BHI is jointly led by Stockholm Resilience Centre (SRC) and Baltic Eye at the Baltic Sea Centre, both at Stockholm University, together with the Ocean Health Index team. The project is funded by Baltic Ecosystem Adaptive Management, Baltic Eye, and the Johansson Family Foundation.

Ocean Health Index

The Ocean Health Index (‘OHI’) is a scientific framework to measure how healthy oceans are. Understanding the state of our oceans is a first step towards ensuring they can continue providing humans benefits now and in the future. Learn more at ohi-science.org.
